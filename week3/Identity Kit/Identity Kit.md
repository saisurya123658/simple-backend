## Style Note

**Fonts:** Poppins for headings and Inter for body text.

**Colors:** #2563EB, #10B981, #111827, #F9FAFB.

**Mood:** Clean, modern, and professional with a focus on readability, allowing projects and technical work to stand out rather than visual decoration.

---

## Design Principles

* Minimal and professional
* Plenty of white space
* Rounded corners
* Consistent spacing
* Simple icons
* No excessive animations
* Accessibility-first color contrast

---

## Brand Summary

This identity kit reflects a modern AI and backend engineering portfolio. The visual style is calm, consistent, and developer-focused, ensuring that projects remain the primary focus while maintaining a polished and professional appearance.
